Centre of Excellence

The COE would support the Governments imperative to set up world-class ecosystem in their Industries to attract investments. Availability of trained manpower and Industry ready workforce is a key missing link towards making the state “Destination of Choice”.
The COE can focus on the following profile of students: improve employability of students and professionals from the state through skill development and innovation / research projects with Industry. Besides, to create environment to foster innovation and entrepreneurship, innovation hub is being completed to incubate the start-ups.
The above COE could also be used for Industry professions to train their work force based on pay per use model and an ecosystem of starts up based on such COE would easily justify the investments in Dassault technologies.

Outsourcing of skilled technical manpower

We have through our COE’s developed a pipe-line of skilled talent, who are deputed across Digital Manufacturing, Mining, Aerospace and allied disciplines across various industries, across the nation and overseas.

Digital Manufacturing Services

Industry 4.0 is focused on creating “smart” environment within the production system. creating intelligent products, processes, and procedures. In the smart factory workers, machines and resources communicate easily.
Digital manufacturing has been considered, over the last decade, as a highly promising set of technologies for reducing product development times and cost as well as for addressing the need for customization, increased product quality, and faster response to the market. This paper describes the evolution of information technology systems in manufacturing, outlining their characteristics and the challenges to be addressed in the future
