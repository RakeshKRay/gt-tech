Dassault System's Global Level Certification

3D Experience platform Explorer
As a 3DEXPERIENCE Platform Explorer - Associate, you will demonstrate collaboration expertise with cutting-edge skills that innovative business seeks out and reward. This exam is a foundational skills test for navigating on the 3DEXPERIENCE Platform to access communities and share content. Including the skills for the visualization and management of project data.

3D Experience Mechanical Designer
As a 3DEXPERIENCE Mechanical Designer - Associate, you have proven your knowledge and skills with CATIA fundamental modelling techniques after initial training and between one to six months of regular practice using CATIA Mechanical Design core products (Part Design, Assembly Design, and Drafting).This exam proposes hands-on challenges based on industrial use cases that will require you to design, modify and analyse parts and assemblies. Fundamental understanding of design intent and drafting principles is assessed in multiple-choice questions
